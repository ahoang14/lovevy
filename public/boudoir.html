Ohohoho there are indeed a few perks that come with being young and pretty. And YES I know about that double-edged sword and YES I did have a brief crisis over it a few months ago.

    Anyways I have a lot of people I can genuinely call my friends now, quality real steel friends. But that's not what's really important. When it comes down to it... can I play pool? The answer is... complicated.
